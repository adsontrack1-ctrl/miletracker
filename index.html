Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 2026

MileTracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use MileTracker:

  • Location data: GPS coordinates used to track trip start/end points and distances. Location is only accessed while the app is in use.
  • Account information: Your name and email address from Google Sign-In.
  • Trip data: Miles driven, trip dates, addresses, and trip categories you assign.
  • App settings: Your preferences such as IRS rate, speed threshold, and notification settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To record and display your trips and mileage statistics
  • To calculate your tax deductions based on IRS standard mileage rates
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase Firestore
  • To send you notifications about trips and reminders (only with your permission)
  • To generate reports you can export or email to your accountant

3. Data Storage & Security

Your trip data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, secured with authentication rules that ensure only you can access your data. We use Google Sign-In for secure authentication. We do not store your data on our own servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessible to you and is stored in your own Firebase account. We may share anonymous, aggregated statistics that do not identify individual users.

5. Location Data

Location data is used solely for mileage tracking. GPS coordinates are used to calculate trip distance and identify start/end addresses. We do not continuously track your location in the background beyond what is needed to detect and record trips.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we have stored about you (available in the app)
  • Delete your data at any time via Settings â†’ Clear All Data
  • Export your data as CSV or PDF at any time
  • Delete your account by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

MileTracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the app's feedback feature or reach out via our support channels.
